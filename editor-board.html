Advisory/Editorial/Reviewer Board Members
Dr. V Tulasi Das

Dr. V. Tulasi Das is a distinguished Professor in the Department of Human Resource Management (HRM) at the MBA program. With a comprehensive academic background and over 28 years of teaching and research experience, Dr. Das is a leading figure in the field of Human Resource Management. Dr. Das holds an impressive array of qualifications, including an M.B.A. from University Campus College, Nagarjuna University, a Master of Human Resource Management (M.H.R.M.) from Acharya Nagarjuna University, where he was awarded a Gold Medal, and a Bachelor of Graduate Law (BGL) from Andhra University. Additionally, he has completed a Postgraduate Diploma in Personnel Management (PGDPM) from Annamalai University, an M.Phil. from Andhra University, and a Ph.D. from the same institution. Dr. Das also holds a National Eligibility Test (NET) qualification in Business Management. His research interests are broad and impactful, encompassing Human Resource Management, Human Resource Development, Strategic Management, and Marketing Management. Dr. Das has supervised the research of 31 Ph.D. candidates and 9 M.Phil. students to completion and is currently guiding 8 Ph.D. scholars. Dr. Das’s scholarly contributions are extensive, with 195 research articles published in national and international journals and 55 book chapters. His editorial work includes editing 15 books and co-authoring 9. He has actively participated in academic conferences, attending 94 national and 47 international seminars, and presenting 88 and 47 papers respectively. Additionally, he has been involved in organizing 20 national events, including serving as Director for 11 and holding other key roles. A highly regarded resource person, Dr. Das has delivered guest lectures and participated in 49 seminars and conferences. His academic work has garnered significant recognition, reflected in his Google Scholar citations, which currently total 162. Dr. Das is also listed in the VIDWAN database with ID 111544, underscoring his prominence in the academic community. Through his extensive research, teaching, and organizational roles, Dr. V. Tulasi Das continues to contribute significantly to the advancement of Human Resource Management and related fields.

Dr. Areman Ramyasri

Dr. Areman Ramyasri is a distinguished academic with extensive qualifications and a strong background in commerce, business management, and information technology. She earned her Ph.D. from the Department of Commerce & Business Management at Kakatiya University in 2022, where her research focused on industrial relations in the Singareni Collieries Company Limited (SCCL). Dr. Ramyasri also holds a Master of Commerce from Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management (GITAM) and an MBA with a specialization in Human Resources from Chaitanya PG College, Kakatiya University. Further enriching her academic credentials, she has completed a Post-Graduation Diploma in Computer Application and a Post-Graduation Diploma in Health Care Management, showcasing her multidisciplinary expertise. Currently, Dr. Ramyasri is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Humanities and Mathematics at G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology & Sciences in Hyderabad. Her teaching repertoire includes subjects such as Managerial Economics, Financial Analysis, and Industrial Management. In addition to her teaching responsibilities, she plays a vital role in institutional and departmental activities, including being a member of the Board of Studies in Management Studies, Faculty Coordinator for the Nirmaan Student Chapter Constitution Club, and Faculty Incharge for departmental libraries and FDP files. Dr. Ramyasri is also an active researcher, with numerous articles published in Scopus-indexed journals and other reputable international journals, focusing on topics like business decision-making, technology in administration, and employee well-being. Her contributions to academia are further highlighted by her involvement in faculty development programs, workshops, and her participation in organizing national-level events.
